FitFlex Gym Management Application

FitFlex is an integrated gym management solution designed for fitness centers to streamline their operations. The application consists of a web-based management interface developed with Symfony and a desktop client for offline use developed with Java 8. It offers features such as member management, class scheduling, payment tracking, and reporting tools to enhance user engagement and simplify gym administration.

  • Member registration and profile management
  • Class and trainer scheduling
  • Real-time attendance tracking
  • Payment tracking and invoices
  • Customizable reports for insights and analytics
